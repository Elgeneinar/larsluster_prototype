About Me

I'm a joke that never gets old

I like to look at myself as a life long student, not because academia is what I'm about, but because I love learning new things. I get invested in all kinds of projects and disciplines, anything creative could captivate me. I love cooking and listening to music, writing or having a deep discussion one on one. Podcasts, games, dance, cinema, I enjoy them all with passion. Those who know me often remark at how diligently I pursue my interests. The world is a fascinating place, if you know how to look at it.

Now I'm in the process of exploring. Traveling the world and learning about new cultures and perspectives is one way to discover the world, another is to take on new projects and ideas. I might go to Argentina and learn to dance tango, or join a comedy club and work on my skills at the improv. These days I'm learning to surf, and working on my writing, while studying computer science. However, at any moment I might take a massage course, join a drum circle or take up Spanish.

But, if you really want to get to know me, let's have a talk or do something!

Get in touch!

If you have an idea, don't hesitate

I'm available for all kinds of projects, be they anything from interviews, shoots, art or writing workshops, collaborations, meet-ups, leading exercises, speaking at events, you name it!

Not sure your gig is right for me? Send me a pitch and we can figgure it out together. I'm always excited to meet new people or get into new kinds of projects! Want me to teach a class on game design? No problem! How about writing for your website? Let's do it! Star in your new movie? I'll learn it! Anything cool you throw at me, I'll give it my best shot. So what are you waiting for?
